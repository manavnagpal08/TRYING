Job Title: Civil Structural Engineer

About Us:
We are a leading company in civil engineering and construction looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning.

Job Summary:
As a Civil Structural Engineer, you will be responsible for designing structural elements, performing structural analysis, and preparing construction documents. You will work closely with civil engineering department to ensure the safety and stability of civil structures.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design structural elements for buildings, bridges, and other civil infrastructure.
- Perform structural analysis using industry-standard software (e.g., SAP2000, ETABS).
- Prepare detailed construction drawings and specifications (AutoCAD, Civil 3D, Revit).
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams.
- Ensure high quality and timely delivery of projects.
- Stay updated with industry best practices and emerging technologies.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Civil Engineering with a focus on Structures.
- X+ years of proven experience in Civil Structural Engineer or a similar role.
- Strong proficiency in Structural Analysis, AutoCAD, Civil 3D, Revit.
- Experience with Concrete Design, Steel Design, Geotechnical Engineering.
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Strong communication and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Project Management, Construction Methods, Building Codes (e.g., IBC).
- Certifications relevant to the role.
- Ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and benefits package.
- Opportunities for professional growth and development.
- Flexible work arrangements.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.
