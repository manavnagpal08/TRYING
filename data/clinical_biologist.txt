Job Title: Clinical Biologist

About Us:
We are a leading company in biomedical research and diagnostics looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning.

Job Summary:
As a Clinical Biologist, you will be responsible for conducting biological experiments, analyzing biological samples, and interpreting clinical data. You will work closely with clinical research laboratory to advance understanding of biological processes and disease mechanisms.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design and conduct biological experiments using techniques such as cell culture, PCR, and Western Blot.
- Analyze biological samples and interpret results for research or diagnostic purposes.
- Maintain accurate laboratory records and contribute to scientific publications.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams.
- Ensure high quality and timely delivery of projects.
- Stay updated with industry best practices and emerging technologies.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Biology, Biochemistry, Molecular Biology, or a related life science.
- X+ years of proven experience in Clinical Biologist or a similar role.
- Strong proficiency in Molecular Biology, Cell Culture, PCR, Western Blot.
- Experience with Microscopy, Bioinformatics, Data Analysis, Laboratory Techniques.
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Strong communication and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Flow Cytometry, ELISA, R or Python for data analysis, GLP/GCP regulations.
- Certifications relevant to the role.
- Ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and benefits package.
- Opportunities for professional growth and development.
- Flexible work arrangements.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.
