Job Title: Cybersecurity Engineer

About Us:
We are a leading company in information security looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning.

Job Summary:
As a Cybersecurity Engineer, you will be responsible for implementing security measures, monitoring for threats, and responding to security incidents. You will work closely with security operations center (SOC) to protect organizational assets from cyber threats.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design, implement, and maintain security systems and controls (firewalls, IDS/IPS).
- Conduct vulnerability assessments and penetration testing.
- Respond to security incidents, analyze root causes, and implement corrective actions.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams.
- Ensure high quality and timely delivery of projects.
- Stay updated with industry best practices and emerging technologies.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Cybersecurity, Computer Science, or a related field.
- X+ years of proven experience in Cybersecurity Engineer or a similar role.
- Strong proficiency in Network Security, Incident Response, Vulnerability Management.
- Experience with SIEM (Security Information and Event Management), Firewalls, Endpoint Protection.
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Strong communication and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Python for scripting, Security Audits, Compliance (e.g., ISO 27001, NIST).
- Certifications relevant to the role.
- Ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and benefits package.
- Opportunities for professional growth and development.
- Flexible work arrangements.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.
