Job Summary:
As a Data Analyst, you will be responsible for collecting, analyzing, and interpreting large datasets to identify trends, patterns, and insights that can help improve business performance. You will work closely with various departments to understand their data needs, develop reports and dashboards, and present your findings in a clear and actionable manner.

Key Responsibilities:

Collect, clean, and preprocess raw data from various sources to ensure data accuracy and completeness.

Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) to identify patterns, anomalies, and relationships within datasets.

Develop and maintain interactive dashboards and reports using tools like Tableau, Power BI, or Looker to visualize key performance indicators (KPIs).

Write complex SQL queries to extract, transform, and load (ETL) data from relational databases.

Utilize statistical methods and programming languages (e.g., Python with Pandas, NumPy, SciPy, or R) to analyze data and build predictive models.

Interpret data analysis results and communicate findings, insights, and recommendations to stakeholders across different departments (e.g., Marketing, Sales, Operations, Product).

Collaborate with data engineers and other teams to improve data collection, data quality, and data warehousing processes.

Identify opportunities for process improvement and operational efficiency through data analysis.

Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices in data analysis, business intelligence, and data science.

Qualifications:

Bachelor's degree in Data Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Economics, or a related quantitative field. Master's degree preferred.

2+ years of proven experience as a Data Analyst or in a similar analytical role.

Strong proficiency in SQL for data extraction and manipulation.

Proficiency in Python (Pandas, NumPy) or R for data analysis and statistical modeling.

Experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau, Power BI, Looker, or similar.

Solid understanding of statistical concepts and experience applying them to real-world problems.

Experience with data cleaning, validation, and preprocessing techniques.

Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.

Strong communication and presentation skills, with the ability to explain complex data insights to non-technical audiences.

Ability to work independently and as part of a collaborative team.

Preferred Qualifications (Bonus Points):

Experience with big data technologies (e.g., Spark, Hadoop, BigQuery, Snowflake).

Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP) and their data services.

Experience with A/B testing and experimental design.

Knowledge of version control systems (e.g., Git).
