Job Title: DevOps Specialist

About Us:
We are a leading company in software delivery automation looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning.

Job Summary:
As a DevOps Specialist, you will be responsible for automating CI/CD pipelines, managing infrastructure as code, and optimizing deployment processes. You will work closely with development and operations teams to accelerate software delivery and improve system reliability.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design, implement, and maintain CI/CD pipelines using Jenkins, GitLab CI, or similar tools.
- Manage cloud infrastructure using Infrastructure as Code (IaC) tools like Terraform and Ansible.
- Implement monitoring and logging solutions (Prometheus, Grafana, ELK Stack) for system health.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams.
- Ensure high quality and timely delivery of projects.
- Stay updated with industry best practices and emerging technologies.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, DevOps, or a related field.
- X+ years of proven experience in DevOps Specialist or a similar role.
- Strong proficiency in CI/CD, Docker, Kubernetes, Cloud Platforms (AWS/Azure/GCP).
- Experience with Terraform, Ansible, Scripting (Python/Bash).
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Strong communication and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Git, Linux, Microservices, System Administration.
- Certifications relevant to the role.
- Ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and benefits package.
- Opportunities for professional growth and development.
- Flexible work arrangements.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.
