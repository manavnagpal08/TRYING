Job Title: Electrical Systems Engineer

About Us:
We are a leading company in electrical engineering looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning.

Job Summary:
As a Electrical Systems Engineer, you will be responsible for designing electrical circuits, developing embedded systems, and testing electrical components. You will work closely with electrical engineering team to develop robust and efficient electrical systems.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design and analyze electrical circuits, including analog and digital components.
- Develop and debug firmware for embedded systems using C/C++.
- Perform testing and validation of electrical systems and components.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams.
- Ensure high quality and timely delivery of projects.
- Stay updated with industry best practices and emerging technologies.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Electrical Engineering or a related field.
- X+ years of proven experience in Electrical Systems Engineer or a similar role.
- Strong proficiency in Circuit Design, Embedded Systems, PCB Layout.
- Experience with Microcontrollers, Signal Processing, Power Electronics.
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Strong communication and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Altium Designer, Eagle, SPICE, LabVIEW.
- Certifications relevant to the role.
- Ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and benefits package.
- Opportunities for professional growth and development.
- Flexible work arrangements.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.
