Job Title: High School Math Teacher

About Us:
We are a leading company in secondary education looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning.

Job Summary:
As a High School Math Teacher, you will be responsible for developing engaging math lessons, instructing students in various math subjects, and assessing student progress. You will work closely with Mathematics Department to foster a love for mathematics and prepare students for future success.

Key Responsibilities:
- Develop and deliver engaging math lessons for high school students (Algebra, Geometry, Calculus).
- Assess student understanding through various methods and provide constructive feedback.
- Collaborate with colleagues, parents, and administrators to support student learning.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams.
- Ensure high quality and timely delivery of projects.
- Stay updated with industry best practices and emerging technologies.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Mathematics Education or a related field.
- X+ years of proven experience in High School Math Teacher or a similar role.
- Strong proficiency in Lesson Planning, Classroom Management, Differentiated Instruction.
- Experience with Algebra, Geometry, Calculus, Student Assessment.
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Strong communication and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Google Classroom, Interactive Whiteboards, Special Education Needs (SEN) strategies.
- Certifications relevant to the role.
- Ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and benefits package.
- Opportunities for professional growth and development.
- Flexible work arrangements.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.
