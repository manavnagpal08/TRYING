Job Title: Mechanical Design Engineer

About Us:
We are a leading company in product design and manufacturing looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning.

Job Summary:
As a Mechanical Design Engineer, you will be responsible for designing mechanical components, performing engineering analysis, and creating technical drawings. You will work closely with engineering design team to develop innovative and reliable mechanical products.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design mechanical components and assemblies using CAD software (SolidWorks, AutoCAD).
- Conduct engineering analysis, including FEA (Finite Element Analysis) and thermodynamics.
- Prepare detailed technical drawings, specifications, and BOMs (Bills of Materials).
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams.
- Ensure high quality and timely delivery of projects.
- Stay updated with industry best practices and emerging technologies.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Mechanical Engineering or a related field.
- X+ years of proven experience in Mechanical Design Engineer or a similar role.
- Strong proficiency in CAD Software (SolidWorks, AutoCAD), FEA, Product Design.
- Experience with Thermodynamics, Materials Science, GD&T (Geometric Dimensioning & Tolerancing).
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Strong communication and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with MATLAB, ANSYS, Prototyping, Manufacturing Processes.
- Certifications relevant to the role.
- Ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and benefits package.
- Opportunities for professional growth and development.
- Flexible work arrangements.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.
