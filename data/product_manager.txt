Job Title: Product Manager

About Us:
We are a leading company in software product development looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to deliver innovative products that solve real user problems.

The Role:
As a Product Manager, you will be responsible for defining product vision and strategy, managing product roadmaps and backlogs, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to bring products to market. You will work closely with product, engineering, and design teams to drive product growth and user satisfaction. This role requires a strong commitment to user empathy and strategic thinking.

Key Responsibilities:
- Conduct market research, competitive analysis, and user interviews to identify opportunities.
- Translate user needs and business goals into clear product requirements and user stories.
- Prioritize features, manage the product backlog, and oversee product launches.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Business, Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field or equivalent practical experience.
- 4+ years of proven professional experience in Product Manager or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in Product Strategy, Roadmap Development, Agile Methodologies (Scrum, Kanban).
- Demonstrated experience with User Research, A/B Testing, Data Analysis, Stakeholder Management.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Jira, Confluence, Figma, SQL, Google Analytics, SaaS product experience.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- shaping the future of our product and impacting millions of users.
