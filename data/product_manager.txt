Job Title: Product Manager

About Us:
We are a leading company in software product development looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning.

Job Summary:
As a Product Manager, you will be responsible for defining product vision and strategy, managing product roadmaps, and gathering user requirements. You will work closely with product and engineering teams to deliver successful products that meet market needs.

Key Responsibilities:
- Define and prioritize product features based on market research and user feedback.
- Translate business requirements into detailed user stories and specifications.
- Collaborate with engineering, design, and marketing teams throughout the product lifecycle.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams.
- Ensure high quality and timely delivery of projects.
- Stay updated with industry best practices and emerging technologies.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Business, Computer Science, or a related field.
- X+ years of proven experience in Product Manager or a similar role.
- Strong proficiency in Product Strategy, Roadmap Development, Agile Methodologies.
- Experience with User Stories, A/B Testing, Market Analysis.
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Strong communication and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Jira, Confluence, Figma, SQL, Google Analytics.
- Certifications relevant to the role.
- Ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and benefits package.
- Opportunities for professional growth and development.
- Flexible work arrangements.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.
