Job Title: Project Coordinator

About Us:
We are a leading company in project management looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning.

Job Summary:
As a Project Coordinator, you will be responsible for assisting with project planning, tracking project progress, and facilitating team communication. You will work closely with project management office (PMO) to ensure projects are completed on time and within budget.

Key Responsibilities:
- Support Project Managers in developing project plans, schedules, and budgets.
- Monitor project progress, identify potential issues, and assist in resolution.
- Organize and facilitate project meetings, prepare agendas, and document minutes.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams.
- Ensure high quality and timely delivery of projects.
- Stay updated with industry best practices and emerging technologies.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Business, Project Management, or a related field.
- X+ years of proven experience in Project Coordinator or a similar role.
- Strong proficiency in Project Planning, Scheduling, Communication Skills.
- Experience with Microsoft Office Suite (Excel, Word, PowerPoint), Project Management Software.
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Strong communication and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Jira, Asana, Smartsheet, Agile Methodologies.
- Certifications relevant to the role.
- Ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and benefits package.
- Opportunities for professional growth and development.
- Flexible work arrangements.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.
