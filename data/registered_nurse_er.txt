Job Title: Registered Nurse (ER)

About Us:
We are a leading company in emergency healthcare looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning.

Job Summary:
As a Registered Nurse (ER), you will be responsible for providing emergency patient care, administering medications, and documenting patient information. You will work closely with Emergency Room (ER) to deliver high-quality, compassionate care to emergency patients.

Key Responsibilities:
- Assess, plan, implement, and evaluate patient care in a fast-paced emergency setting.
- Administer medications and treatments as prescribed, monitoring patient responses.
- Document all patient care activities accurately and timely in electronic health records (EHR).
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams.
- Ensure high quality and timely delivery of projects.
- Stay updated with industry best practices and emerging technologies.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Nursing (ADN or BSN).
- X+ years of proven experience in Registered Nurse (ER) or a similar role.
- Strong proficiency in Emergency Patient Care, Triage, Medication Administration.
- Experience with BLS/ACLS Certification, Critical Thinking, Electronic Health Records (EHR).
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Strong communication and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with PALS Certification, Trauma Nursing, Crisis Intervention, Communication Skills.
- Certifications relevant to the role.
- Ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and benefits package.
- Opportunities for professional growth and development.
- Flexible work arrangements.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.
