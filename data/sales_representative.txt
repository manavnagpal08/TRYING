Job Title: Sales Representative

About Us:
We are a leading company in B2B sales looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning.

Job Summary:
As a Sales Representative, you will be responsible for generating new leads, presenting product solutions, and closing deals. You will work closely with sales team to exceed sales quotas and expand customer base.

Key Responsibilities:
- Identify and qualify new sales opportunities through prospecting and outreach.
- Conduct compelling product demonstrations and presentations to potential clients.
- Negotiate contracts and close sales to achieve revenue targets.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams.
- Ensure high quality and timely delivery of projects.
- Stay updated with industry best practices and emerging technologies.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Business, Sales, or a related field.
- X+ years of proven experience in Sales Representative or a similar role.
- Strong proficiency in Sales Acumen, Lead Generation, CRM Software (e.g., Salesforce).
- Experience with Negotiation, Presentation Skills, Client Relationship Management.
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Strong communication and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Cold Calling, Sales Forecasting, Microsoft Office Suite.
- Certifications relevant to the role.
- Ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and benefits package.
- Opportunities for professional growth and development.
- Flexible work arrangements.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.
