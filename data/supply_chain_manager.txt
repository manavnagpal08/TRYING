Job Title: Supply Chain Manager

About Us:
We are a leading company in supply chain and logistics looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning.

Job Summary:
As a Supply Chain Manager, you will be responsible for overseeing end-to-end supply chain operations, optimizing inventory levels, and managing supplier relationships. You will work closely with supply chain team to ensure efficient and cost-effective flow of goods and services.

Key Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement supply chain strategies to improve efficiency and reduce costs.
- Manage inventory levels, demand forecasting, and logistics operations.
- Negotiate contracts with suppliers and manage vendor performance.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams.
- Ensure high quality and timely delivery of projects.
- Stay updated with industry best practices and emerging technologies.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Supply Chain Management, Logistics, or Business Administration.
- X+ years of proven experience in Supply Chain Manager or a similar role.
- Strong proficiency in Supply Chain Management, Logistics, Inventory Optimization.
- Experience with Demand Planning, Supplier Management, ERP Systems (e.g., SAP, Oracle SCM).
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Strong communication and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Data Analysis (Excel, SQL), Lean Six Sigma, Project Management.
- Certifications relevant to the role.
- Ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and benefits package.
- Opportunities for professional growth and development.
- Flexible work arrangements.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.
