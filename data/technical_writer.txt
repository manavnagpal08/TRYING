Job Title: Technical Writer

About Us:
We are a leading company in technical documentation looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning.

Job Summary:
As a Technical Writer, you will be responsible for creating user manuals, developing API documentation, and maintaining knowledge bases. You will work closely with product and engineering teams to produce clear, concise, and accurate technical content.

Key Responsibilities:
- Write, edit, and maintain high-quality technical documentation, including user guides and release notes.
- Collaborate with subject matter experts to gather information and ensure accuracy.
- Organize and structure content for optimal readability and user experience.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams.
- Ensure high quality and timely delivery of projects.
- Stay updated with industry best practices and emerging technologies.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Technical Communication, English, or Computer Science.
- X+ years of proven experience in Technical Writer or a similar role.
- Strong proficiency in Technical Writing, Documentation, Content Management Systems (CMS).
- Experience with API Documentation, Markdown, XML/HTML.
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Strong communication and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with MadCap Flare, Confluence, Git, Adobe FrameMaker.
- Certifications relevant to the role.
- Ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and benefits package.
- Opportunities for professional growth and development.
- Flexible work arrangements.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.
