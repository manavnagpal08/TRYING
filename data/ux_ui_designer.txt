Job Title: UX/UI Designer

About Us:
We are a leading company in digital product design looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning.

Job Summary:
As a UX/UI Designer, you will be responsible for conducting user research, creating wireframes and prototypes, and designing intuitive user interfaces. You will work closely with design and product teams to enhance user experience and product usability.

Key Responsibilities:
- Conduct user research, usability testing, and analyze user feedback.
- Create wireframes, storyboards, user flows, and prototypes using design tools.
- Design visually appealing and intuitive user interfaces (UI) for web and mobile applications.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams.
- Ensure high quality and timely delivery of projects.
- Stay updated with industry best practices and emerging technologies.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Interaction Design, Graphic Design, or a related field.
- X+ years of proven experience in UX/UI Designer or a similar role.
- Strong proficiency in User Research, Wireframing, Prototyping, UI Design.
- Experience with Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD, Usability Testing.
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Strong communication and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Design Systems, Accessibility Standards.
- Certifications relevant to the role.
- Ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and benefits package.
- Opportunities for professional growth and development.
- Flexible work arrangements.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.
